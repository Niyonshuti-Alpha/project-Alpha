Banana

Bananas are one of the most popular and widely consumed fruits worldwide. Known for their distinct yellow peel and creamy, sweet interior, bananas are enjoyed fresh, in desserts, and as a versatile ingredient in many recipes.

Nutritional Benefits

Bananas are rich in potassium, fiber, vitamin C, and vitamin B6, making them beneficial for heart health, digestion, and energy. Their natural sugars provide a quick energy boost, making bananas a popular snack for active individuals.
