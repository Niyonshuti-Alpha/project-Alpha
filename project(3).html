Tomato

The tomato is a popular fruit with a juicy, tangy taste, widely used in cuisines worldwide. Typically red, tomatoes can also come in colors like yellow, orange, green, and even purple. They are highly versatile and add flavor and color to various dishes.

Nutritional Benefits

Tomatoes are an excellent source of vitamins C and K, potassium, and folate. They are also rich in antioxidants, particularly lycopene, which has been linked to heart health and reduced cancer risk. Low in calories, tomatoes are a great addition to a balanced diet.

Uses

Tomatoes are enjoyed fresh in salads, cooked into sauces, added to soups, and used as a base for countless recipes. Their vibrant color and flavor make them a staple in Mediterranean, Italian, and Latin American cuisines.
